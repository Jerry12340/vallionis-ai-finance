Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use StockAI:

  • Account Data: Email address, password (hashed), subscription status
  • Investment Preferences: Risk tolerance, time horizon, sector focus
  • Payment Data: Processed securely by Stripe (we never store credit card details)
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, timestamps (for analytics and security)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us provide and improve our services:

  • Generate personalized stock recommendations
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Improve our machine learning models (anonymized where possible)
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity

3. Data Sharing

We only share data with trusted third parties when necessary:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing (View Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Market Data Providers: Finnhub, Yahoo Finance for stock market data
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law (e.g., subpoenas, court orders)

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Your Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Access or request a copy of your personal data
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Contact us at vallionisai@gmail.com for data requests.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Limited access to personal data by our team

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • Maintaining user sessions
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Basic analytics to improve our service

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.
